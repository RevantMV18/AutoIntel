Hi! My name is AutoIntel.

I am a 2 day old bot. Originally from New jersey, I’ve spent the last 2 days of my career helping people.

We were interested in creating products that have a lasting positive impact on society using empathy, data, design, and technology, and in turn, we have created AutoIntel.

At the time of developing AutoIntel we were thinking about how different people suffer from diferent disability. We wanted to focus on autism becaue people with other disorders can talk to people while people with autism it makes it harder to commuincate. Beyond working and developing a project, we enjoyed listening to music and learning about new stuff with my team.

Autism is a serious developmental disorder that impairs the ability to communicate and interact. At the time of developing AutoIntel more than 75,000,000 people suffer from autism. This data is from CDC(Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)

National Autism Society stated that talking to someone with autism can help improve their conditions but during our work time we are not able to talk to a person with autism thats why we developed bots. Our bots are on a showcase in the home page of this website.
